What is this blog all about?

Dec 15, 2017 1 min read General

Welcome to my blog!

As you may already know (or not), my name is Anderson Neisse, I am a statistician and an enthusiast of natural sciences and technology. In fact, the only thing that kept me from graduating at physics instead of statistics was the fact that statistics and data analysis are the most awesome things in this world (and the fact that I like programming, of course)!

Well, apart from that, I like to analyze interesting data, especially the ones with potential to underline amazing behaviours. That said, no wonder why I am into such interests as Statistics, Data Science and Machine Learning, right?

What about the blog…

Well, this blog is basically a way to share some analyses that I’ll perform from now on. Most analyses that I do in my spare time will consist of Kaggle’s competitions and kernels, so this blog will basically host analyses that doesn’t fit on Kaggle. For instance, a WhatsApp group chat data analysis that I am planning to post in the near future, such data cannot be shared, only the analysis. I may also post about worthy-posting research that I’ll be doing during my master degree from 2018 to 2020.
